Overview

Purpose

Our purpose? To make people want to hire our company to take them on a white water rafting trip. We need to give the information necessary so they know that we are trained professionals, they will have fun, and be safe while doing it. The process is easy. We provide the gear, the guides, transportation, and the know how for a unforgetable adventure.

Audience

Who are the target customers?

Our adudience is people age 25-45 year olds, middle and upper-middle class with disposable income, who have an interest in the outdoors, water sports, adventure, fitness, nature, and adrenaline sports.

They could be single, or married with children professionals who live in urban or suburban areas. They are interested in a weekend get away or a vacation adventure.

Or they could be businesses who want team building activities or rewards for their employees.

What do our customers want?

This is what the customers want:

  • An exciting and safe white water rafting trip
  • In a beautiful natural setting
  • Professional, knowledgeable, and friendly guides
  • Customizable packages that will meet their needs
  • An easy booking and paying experience

What do they expect from the website?

The website should have the following:

  • Information on rafting packages along with pricing and booking options
  • Safety measures and guide certification
  • Customer reviews and comments
  • Photo gallery
  • FAQ on what to bring, what to wear, difficulty levels, what to expect your first time, information about children on the trip, etc.
  • Contact and location information

What are the target audiences unmet needs?

The following are some of the target audiences unmet needs:

  • Easy booking process
  • Flexible packages that actually meet their needs (different levels of difficulty)
  • Reasonable pricing
  • Trip prep guide

How will my audience usually access my site?

Some will use a mobile device to access the site, and others will access it using a computer.
