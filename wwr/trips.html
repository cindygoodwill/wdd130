White Water Rafting Packages

Discover Idaho’s rivers like never before—only with River Runners, your ultimate rafting experience awaits!

Contact Us to schedule one of the trips below.

Salmon River a.k.a. The River of No Return!

Experience the thrilling rapids of the Salmon River, also known as The River of No Return. This trip offers an exhilarating adventure through breathtaking landscapes, perfect for those seeking a challenging white water rafting experience.

Salmon River 3-days

Embark on a three-day journey down the Salmon River, navigating its intense rapids and enjoying serene camping spots along the way. This extended trip provides a perfect blend of adventure and relaxation in a stunning natural setting.

Snake River

The Snake River offers an exciting white water rafting experience with varied trip durations. Whether you choose a half-day or a full-day adventure, you’ll be captivated by the powerful rapids and beautiful scenery.

Snake River 2-days

Join us for a two-day rafting trip on the Snake River, where you’ll tackle challenging rapids and camp under the stars. This trip is perfect for thrill-seekers looking for an immersive and adventurous experience.

Lochsa River

For the ultimate white water rafting challenge, the Lochsa River is your destination. With Class V rapids, this trip is designed for expert rafters seeking an intense and adrenaline-pumping adventure.

Payette River

The Payette River offers a more relaxed rafting experience, ideal for beginners and families. Enjoy a scenic and fun-filled day on the water with varying trip lengths to suit your schedule and comfort level.
